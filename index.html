About the Institution

Sri Balavinayakar Natyalaya is a prestigious Bharatanatyam dance institution dedicated to preserving and spreading the divine art of Bharatanatyam. With a commitment to discipline, tradition, and artistic excellence, the institution has nurtured numerous students and guided them towards mastery in this ancient classical dance form. Through years of dedicated training, students develop not only technical finesse but also a deep spiritual connection to the art. The institution continues to uphold the rich cultural heritage of Bharatanatyam, inspiring future generations to carry forward its timeless beauty.

A Tribute to Our Guru - Smt. Rajalakshmi Senthilkumar

Smt. Rajalakshmi Senthilkumar


Smt. Rajalakshmi Senthilkumar, the esteemed founder and guru of Sri Balavinayakar Natyalaya, was a beacon of dedication and passion in Bharatanatyam. Under her meticulous guidance, countless students flourished, embracing both the technical and spiritual essence of this sacred art. Her teachings went beyond dance; she instilled values of discipline, perseverance, and respect for tradition in every student. The foundation she built continues to inspire generations, leaving an indelible mark on the world of Bharatanatyam.


With unwavering patience and a deep-rooted love for Bharatanatyam, she transformed her students into skilled dancers, instilling in them the discipline, grace, and devotion required to carry forward this tradition. Every Salangai Poojai and Arangetram was a testament to her relentless efforts, making Sri Balavinayakar Natyalaya a symbol of excellence. Her dedication was evident in every performance, where her students embodied the beauty and depth of Bharatanatyam. Today, her legacy continues to thrive, illuminating the path for aspiring artists.





Continuing the Legacy - Akshaya Senthilkumar


After the unfortunate passing of our beloved guru, Smt. Rajalakshmi Senthilkumar, her daughter Akshaya Senthilkumar has taken up the responsibility of leading Sri Balavinayakar Natyalaya. With the same commitment and dedication as her mother, Akshaya continues to guide students with passion and devotion, ensuring that the rich traditions of Bharatanatyam are carried forward with excellence. She has introduced innovative teaching methods while preserving the authenticity of the art form. Through her leadership, the institution continues to grow, fostering talent and shaping the future of Bharatanatyam.
